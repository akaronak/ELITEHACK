1. Introduction

Mensa ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the Mensa mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of your information.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our App. This policy outlines how we handle your personal and health-related data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Camera Permission

Purpose: The App requests camera access to allow users to capture photos for medical reports and health documentation.

  • Usage: Camera data is only used when you explicitly choose to take photos within the App
  • Storage: Photos are stored locally on your device and can be uploaded to our secure servers only with your consent
  • Deletion: You can delete photos at any time from the App

2.2 Audio Permission

Purpose: Audio recording is used for voice notes and health-related voice dumps.

  • Usage: Audio is recorded only when you initiate recording within the App
  • Storage: Audio files are stored locally and encrypted
  • Encryption: All audio data is encrypted both in transit and at rest

2.3 Location Data

Purpose: Location data may be collected for health tracking and appointment reminders.

  • Usage: Location is only accessed when you enable location services for specific features
  • Control: You can disable location access at any time through your device settings

2.4 Health Information

Purpose: The App collects menstrual cycle data, health metrics, and medical history.

  • Usage: This data is used to provide personalized health insights and reminders
  • Storage: All health data is encrypted and stored securely on our servers
  • Sensitivity: We treat all health information with the highest level of confidentiality

2.5 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Name and age
  • Emergency contact information
  • Medical history (optional)

2.6 Device Information

We may collect:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Unique device identifiers

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide and improve the App's functionality
  • Health Tracking: To send health reminders and notifications
  • Insights: To generate health reports and personalized insights
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations
  • Safety: To protect user safety and security
  • Communication: To send important updates about the App
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with the App (anonymized)

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: End-to-end encryption for sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Authentication: Secure authentication mechanisms including two-factor authentication
  • Audits: Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access Control: Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Compliance: Compliance with data protection regulations
⚠️ Important: While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your login credentials confidential.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your personal health information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share your data
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Healthcare Providers: With healthcare providers you authorize
  • Service Providers: With service providers who assist us (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Business Transfer: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Right to access your personal data
  • Correction: Right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Right to request deletion of your data (Right to be Forgotten)
  • Opt-out: Right to opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Export: Right to export your health data in a portable format
  • Restriction: Right to restrict processing of your data
  • Objection: Right to object to certain processing activities

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@mensahealth.app

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Health data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Deletion Request: You can request deletion of your data at any time
  • Permanent Removal: Deleted data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days
  • Backups: Backup copies may be retained for up to 90 days for disaster recovery
  • Legal Hold: Data may be retained longer if required by law

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.

Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information to us should contact us immediately at privacy@mensahealth.app

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of significant changes via:

  • Email notification to your registered email address
  • In-app notification
  • Prominent notice on our website

Your continued use of the App after changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
