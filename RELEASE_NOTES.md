## ✨ What's New in v1.0.0

### 🚀 Core Features

#### 📊 Multi-Tracker Health Logging
- **Menstruation Tracking**: Log flow levels, symptoms, moods, and cycle patterns
- **Pregnancy Tracking**: Monitor weekly progress, health metrics, and symptoms
- **Menopause Tracking**: Track symptoms and wellness during menopause transition
- Automatic cycle predictions based on your logs
- Beautiful cycle calendar with historical data

#### 🤖 AI-Powered Health Companion
- **Gemini AI Integration**: Personalized health insights based on YOUR data
- **Personalized Notifications**: Smart reminders tailored to your cycle phase
- **Cute Period Reminders**: AI-generated loving messages based on your health patterns
- **Agora Voice Agent**: Talk to your health companion with two modes:
  - 📚 **Education Mode**: Learn about health topics
  - 💬 **Ranting Mode**: Emotional support and venting space

#### 💬 Smart Notifications
- **WhatsApp Integration**: Receive health reminders directly on WhatsApp
- **Local Notifications**: Instant in-app alerts
- **Push Notifications**: Firebase Cloud Messaging support
- **Multi-Channel Delivery**: Choose how you want to be notified

#### 🎯 Gamification & Rewards
- **Daily Streak System**: Track consecutive days of logging
- **Wallet Points**: Earn points for consistent tracking
- **Voucher Rewards**: Redeem points for exclusive rewards
- **Achievement Badges**: Celebrate your health journey milestones

#### 🎨 Personalization
- **Theme Support**: Light and Dark modes
- **Multiple Color Schemes**: Purple, Rose, Teal, Amber, Indigo
- **Multi-Language Support**: English and Hindi
- **Customizable Trackers**: Choose your primary health tracker

#### 🔐 Privacy & Security
- **End-to-End Encryption**: Your data is protected
- **No Third-Party Sharing**: Your information stays with you
- **One-Tap Account Deletion**: Complete data removal on demand
- **Secure Authentication**: Firebase-backed security

- Three beautiful introduction slides
