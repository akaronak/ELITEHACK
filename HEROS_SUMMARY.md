# HerOS: Complete Vision Summary
## From Mensa to "Her Body, Her World, Her Rules"

---

## WHAT IS HerOS?

HerOS is a complete life OS for women that covers every stage from childhood (8) to menopause (50+). It's not just a period tracker—it's a personal health companion that:

✅ **Knows her life stage** and adapts accordingly  
✅ **Detects chronic conditions** early (PCOS, Endo, Perimenopause)  
✅ **Makes logging fun** (6 different modes, sarcastic responses)  
✅ **Generates her personal manual** (Body Protocol)  
✅ **Prevents burnout** (Burnout Radar)  
✅ **Builds community** (Anonymous experiments)  
✅ **Feels alive** (Twin animations, personalized insights)  
✅ **Respects privacy** (Ollama runs locally)  

---

## THE 9 LIFE STAGES

| Stage | Age | Vibe | Focus |
|-------|-----|------|-------|
| 1 | 8-12 | Friendly curiosity | Body changes, learning |
| 2 | 12-16 | Real talk, witty | Periods, sarcasm, empowerment |
| 3 | 16-25 | Sarcastic, strategic | Adulting, work-life balance |
| 4 | 18-40 | Hopeful, strategic | Fertility, trying for baby |
| 5 | Pregnancy | Excited, nervous | Baby development, health |
| 6 | Postpartum | Real, compassionate | Recovery, mental health |
| 7 | 25-40 | Sarcastic, burnout-alert | Work, parenting, chaos |
| 8 | 35-55 | Detective mode | Perimenopause, hot flashes |
| 9 | 50+ | Wise, self-care | Menopause, long-term health |

## KEY FEATURES

### 1. Interactive Logging (6 Modes)
- **Wheel Spin**: Loot box style random questions
- **Chat**: Conversational with sarcastic AI
- **Body Selfie**: Emoji/sticker reactions
- **Drag Twin**: Avatar slider for pain/mood
- **Voice Dump**: 10-second voice recording
- **Auto-Guess**: Pattern prediction

### 2. Instant Feedback
- Sarcastic insights after every log
- Twin animations that react
- Streak badges with meaning
- Contextual nudges

### 3. Chronic Condition Detection
- **PCOS/PCOD**: Pattern detection + lifestyle tips
- **Endometriosis**: Pain mapping + specialist recommendations
- **Perimenopause**: Hot flash tracking + HRT monitoring

### 4. Body Protocol (Auto-Generated Manual)
- Personal patterns discovered
- Helper strategies that work for her
- Red flags & SOS kit
- Work-around recommendations
- Exportable as PDF

### 5. Day Editor (Smart Scheduling)
- Predicts good/bad days
- Suggests task scheduling
- Recommends break timing
- Adjusts for conditions

### 6. Burnout Radar
- Detects burnout early
- Stress heatmap
- Safety flags
- Urgent intervention prompts

### 7. Community Experiments
- Weekly 7-day challenges
- Monthly 30-day deep dives
- Anonymous participation
- Real-time results aggregation

### 8. Multi-Language Support
- English, Hindi, Tamil, Bengali, Marathi, Telugu, Gujarati
- Language-specific tone variations
- Culturally responsive

### 9. Accessibility First
- Dyslexia-friendly font
- Font size slider (12px-28px)
- High-contrast mode
- Animation toggle
- Screen reader compatible
