## 📝 Full Description

### We Listen. We Don't Judge. Your Secrets Stay Yours. 💜

**Mensa** is your personal women's health companion designed with care, privacy, and empowerment at its core. Track your menstruation, pregnancy, or menopause journey with personalized AI insights, beautiful cycle predictions, and supportive features.

### ✨ What Makes Mensa Special

🌸 **Multi-Tracker Support**
- Menstruation tracking with accurate cycle predictions
- Pregnancy tracking with weekly progress monitoring
- Menopause tracking with symptom management
- Comprehensive health logging and analysis

🤖 **AI-Powered Intelligence**
- Personalized health insights using Google Gemini AI
- Cute, loving period reminders based on YOUR data
- Smart notifications tailored to your cycle phase
- Voice-based health companion with emotional support

💬 **Smart Notifications**
- WhatsApp reminders for your health updates
- Local notifications for quick alerts
- Push notifications via Firebase
- Customizable notification preferences

🎯 **Gamification & Rewards**
- Daily streak system to track consistency
- Wallet points for logging your health
- Voucher rewards for your dedication
- Achievement badges for milestones

🎨 **Personalization**
- Light and Dark themes
- Multiple color schemes (Purple, Rose, Teal, Amber, Indigo)
- English and Hindi language support
- Customizable health tracker selection

🔐 **Privacy & Security**
- End-to-end encryption for your data
- No third-party data sharing
- One-tap account deletion
- Complete data control

**Menopause Tracking**
