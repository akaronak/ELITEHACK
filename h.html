# Privacy Policy for Mensa - Women's Health **Last Updated: December 2025** ## Introduction Mensa ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the Mensa mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of your information. ## 1. Information We Collect ### 1.1 Camera Permission - **Purpose**: The App requests camera access to allow users to capture photos for medical reports and health documentation. - **Usage**: Camera data is only used when you explicitly choose to take photos within the App. - **Storage**: Photos are stored locally on your device and can be uploaded to our secure servers only with your consent. ### 1.2 Audio Permission - **Purpose**: Audio recording is used for voice notes and health-related voice dumps. - **Usage**: Audio is recorded only when you initiate recording within the App. - **Storage**: Audio files are stored locally and encrypted. ### 1.3 Location Data - **Purpose**: Location data may be collected for health tracking and appointment reminders. - **Usage**: Location is only accessed when you enable location services for specific features. ### 1.4 Health Information - **Purpose**: The App collects menstrual cycle data, health metrics, and medical history. - **Usage**: This data is used to provide personalized health insights and reminders. - **Storage**: All health data is encrypted and stored securely. ## 2. How We Use Your Information - To provide and improve the App's functionality - To send health reminders and notifications - To generate health reports and insights - To comply with legal obligations - To protect user safety and security ## 3. Data Security We implement industry-standard security measures including: - End-to-end encryption for sensitive data - Secure authentication mechanisms - Regular security audits - Compliance with data protection regulations ## 4. Data Sharing We do **not** share your personal health information with third parties except: - With your explicit consent - When required by law - With healthcare providers you authorize - With service providers who assist us (under strict confidentiality agreements) ## 5. Your Rights You have the right to: - Access your personal data - Request correction of inaccurate data - Request deletion of your data - Opt-out of non-essential communications - Export your health data ## 6. Data Retention - Health data is retained as long as your account is active - You can request deletion of your data at any time - Deleted data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days ## 7. Children's Privacy The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. ## 8. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via the App or email. ## 9. Compliance This App complies with: - GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) - CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) - HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act) - Local data protection laws --- **By using the Mensa App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.**
